SECTION 3b

Group Nudge Interaction (Cart-Aware Priority)

Shows how nudges switch between workation/coliving and group based on cart state. 0 beds = date-based nudge, 2+ beds (within group day_range) = group nudge.

Cart-Aware Priority Waterfall

  1. Cart empty (0 beds) → Show workation/coliving nudge (date-based)
  2. Cart has 2+ beds AND group-eligible AND nights within day_range (1-3) → Switch to group nudge (bed-based)
  3. Cart has 2+ beds but NOT group-eligible → Continue showing workation/coliving nudge
  4. Cart has 1 bed → Continue showing workation/coliving nudge (single bed ≠ group)

Group discount applies on top of workation/coliving pricing — it's a coupon-based discount, not a replacement. The nudge switch is purely about which message to show.
